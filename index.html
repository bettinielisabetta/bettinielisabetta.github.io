Mechanical Engineer specializing in structural dynamics, mechanism design, and high-fidelity FEM modeling, currently at NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory. Background includes design, kinematic and singularity analysis of multi-DoF linkages, hyperelastic FEM modeling of soft robotic components, and actuator sizing via simulation under worst-case loading conditions. Experienced in mechanical prototyping and hardware integration, including sensors and actuators, as well as modal analysis and FEM–experimental correlation for precision mechanical systems.
